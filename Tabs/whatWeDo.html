What We Do

Legacy-Builder Program – we’ve leveraged our extensive experience in the life coaching space to create a legacy building program that can be tailored to cover the client’s change agenda, needs, values and results. Using a host of resources, we help our clients to assess, identify, create and pursue a successful legacy as well as refocus their present life for greater energy, fulfillment and productivity. Our coaching style brings structure to your process of thinking, planning, deciding and doing. Instead of just talking about your needs, we assist you to systematically walk through a framework that assits you to clarify an objective, explore new options, make firm decisions and become accountable to act on your choices. Legacy Readiness- gaining clarity on the type of legacy you want begins with connecting or reconnecting with the real you. We work to ensure that our clients connect with their passions and life purpose and gain an understanding of their unique identity. Among our many coaching offerings are- Innate Design Core Passion Dream Inventory Ideal Life Tracker Qualification Audit Destiny Discovery Difference Maker Legacy Creation- at YDLC, we provide candid and prudent support and encouragement to our clients as they design a road-map to achieve their legacy goals. We give our clients the tools necessary to make informed and thoughtful decisions on how to create their desired legacy. Among our many coaching offerings are- Passions and Values Strategic Positioning Executive Coaching Legacy Management- through strategic coaching, we assist our clients to manage reputation risks and wide-ranging crises that have the potential to derail their legacies. Among our many coaching offerings are- Response coaching Message internal and external message development Damage assessment Legacy recovery Legacy Development- our passion for legacy building is founded on the premise that its not a question of if you are building a legacy but how you are building one. We also believe that its never too early or too late to begin. In this regard, YDLC provides educational institutions and corporations with customized training modules on legacy development. Our training modules are tailored to meet each client’s specific needs. Previous training programs include: Relationship management Transformational Coaching Career development and branding
